About

Hello! I'm Daisy.

I am a web developer from Arizona with a passion for creating functional, dynamic, and appealing websites. I aim to utilize my skillsets in future projects and opportunities that present themselves.

Through the coding program provided by the Western Maricopa Education Center, I earned the Microsoft Technology Associate certification. I consider myself a motivated, reliable, and detail-oriented coder, which is present in my work and in person. Moreover, I have creativity within me that is expressed through wild ideas that are then transferred to the screen.

Outside of my academic life, I spend time volunteering at a variety of places, such as shelters and soup kitchens, as I understand the importance of giving back. I enjoy reading books on social issues and make it a point to educate myself on current events.
